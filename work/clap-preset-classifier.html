CLAP Preset Classifier

Contrastive audio-text synth-preset classification using DawDreamer for headless rendering, trained on a custom tagged dataset.

Overview

The project learns a shared embedding space for synth sounds and the words that describe them, so a preset can be tagged — or searched — by natural-language descriptions of how it sounds.

Pipeline

DawDreamer renders synth presets headlessly at scale to build the audio side of the dataset, paired with curated text tags. A CLAP-style contrastive objective then aligns audio and text embeddings for classification and retrieval.
