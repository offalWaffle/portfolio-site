Patch Matcher

A Synplant-style genetic algorithm applied to physical-modelling synthesis — perceptual loss, CMA-ES evolutionary search, and a differentiable polish stage.

Overview

Given a target sound, Patch Matcher searches a physical-modelling synth's parameter space to reproduce it. An evolutionary search explores broadly, guided by a perceptual loss that compares sounds the way a listener would rather than sample-by-sample.

Approach

CMA-ES drives the global search toward promising regions of the parameter space, then a differentiable polish stage refines the best candidates by gradient descent. The combination balances broad exploration with precise convergence.

Details

  • Physical modelling
  • CMA-ES
  • Perceptual loss
  • Differentiable DSP
